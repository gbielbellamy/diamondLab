Research

  • IN VITRO MODELS
    OF HUMAN MICROBIOME

    We want to address the complexity and diversity of human microbiomes through the development and the genome-resolved metagenomic characterization of in vitro systems that better reflect host microbiomes and responses, allowing extensive research without the need of in vivo studies.

  • COMMUNITY
    EDITING

    Our lab is involved in a large IGI collaborative effort to develop tools for in situ targeted genetic modification and interrogation of microbial communities known as BiomeForge.

  • CYANOBACTERIAL
    COMMUNITIES

    We are interested in the development of model cyanobacterial community systems and targeted community editing tools to interrogate the functions of these systems in the context of environmental sustainability and human health.

Team

Our Lab

       We are a dedicated team of scientists passionate about unraveling the microbial world, comprising multidisciplinary researchers from a broad range of fields. Our collaborative efforts focus on exploring the intricate dynamics of microbial communities, their roles in human health and disease, and their potential applications in biotechnology and environmental remediation.We employ various methods, including genome-resolved metagenomics, in vitro microbial modeling and bioinformatics, to advance our research.
